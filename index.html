NooneWeone

(from No one — individual dissolution, We one — unified consciousness)
“NooneWeone” means: (No one We One) : when no one remains, We One begins.
Core Proposition
Change of consciousness is to reconstruct meaning.
To awaken is not to rise, but to reorganize meaning.
To find why you were born—
and when you find it, to smile, and quietly close your eyes.

Speak not of the answer;
let the next one begin from your silence.

For in the silence of one,
We begin anew.
